# Privacy Policy for Forq

**Last Updated: January 14, 2026**

## Introduction

Welcome to Forq ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Service").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing or using the Service, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use the Service.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information You Provide

When you register for and use our Service, we collect the following information:

#### Account Information
- **Username**: A unique identifier you choose for your account
- **Email Address**: Used for account identification and communication
- **Password**: Stored as a securely hashed value (we never store plain-text passwords)
- **First Name and Last Name** (optional): To personalize your experience
- **Profile Picture** (optional): To customize your profile

#### Nutritional Goals and Health Information
- **Daily Calorie Goals**: Your target daily caloric intake
- **Macronutrient Goals**: Your target protein, carbohydrate, and fat intake
- **Food Logs**: Records of meals you log, including:
  - Meal type (breakfast, lunch, dinner, snack)
  - Food items consumed
  - Serving sizes and quantities
  - Date and time of logging
  - Optional notes about meals
  - Calculated nutritional totals

#### Food Database Information
- **Custom Food Entries**: Food items you create and add to your personal database
- **Favorite Foods**: Foods you save for quick access
- **Barcode Scans**: When you scan product barcodes to add foods

### 2. Automatically Collected Information

#### Usage Data
- **Account Creation and Update Timestamps**: When you create and modify your account
- **Food Log Timestamps**: When you log meals and foods
- **API Request Data**: Information about your interactions with our servers

#### Device Information
- **Device Type and Operating System**: Information about your mobile device
- **Application Version**: The version of the Forq app you're using

## Third-Party Services

### FatSecret Platform API

We integrate with the FatSecret Platform API to provide comprehensive food nutritional information. When you search for foods or scan barcodes:

- Your search queries are sent to FatSecret's servers
- FatSecret may collect information about these requests according to their own privacy policy
- We recommend reviewing [FatSecret's Privacy Policy](https://www.fatsecret.com/privacy) for more information

**Important Note**: We do not share your personal account information, user profile, or complete food logs with FatSecret. Only search queries and barcode data necessary for food lookups are transmitted.

We use the information we collect to:

2. **Improve Our Service**
   - Analyze usage patterns to improve app functionality
   - Debug and fix technical issues
   - Develop new features based on user needs

3. **Communicate With You**
   - Send important updates about the Service
   - Respond to your inquiries and support requests

4. **Ensure Security**
   - Detect and prevent fraudulent activities

   - Maintain data integrity

## Data Storage and Security

### Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

- **Password Hashing**: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with salt rounds before storage
- **Secure Database**: All data is stored in a secure MySQL database
- **Server Security**: Our API servers implement authentication and access controls
- **Data Transmission**: Communications between your device and our servers use secure protocols

### Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the Service. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.
